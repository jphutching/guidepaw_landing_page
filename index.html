What it does

GuidePaw keeps the daily workflow organized without turning it into a hunt. The home path stays short, and the deeper tools are still there when you need them.

Training logs that stay readable

Capture sessions, edit history, and media review in one place so progress is easy to follow.

Access and identity under control

Dog access, public QR pages, and contact fallback data stay tied to the right handler.

Care and reminders without clutter

Appointments, medications, wearable snapshots, and notifications stay visible but not noisy.
